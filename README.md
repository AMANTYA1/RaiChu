### Commands ğŸ› 
#### For all in group

- `/vplay` - reply to youtube url or song file to play video
#### Admins only
- `/pause` - pause song play
- `/resume` - resume song play
- `/skip` - play next song
- `/end` - stop music play
- `/vstop` - stop video stream
- `/vpause` - pause video stream
- `/vresume` - resume video stream
- `/vskip` - skip video stream
